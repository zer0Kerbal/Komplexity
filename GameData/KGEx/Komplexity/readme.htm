Komplexity (KPLX)

for Custom Barn Kit

by @zer0Kerbal

A Custom Barn Kit config addon that increases Kerbal Space Center building levels to ten levels instead of the stock game's four. For Kerbal Space Program. Doesn't add more building models.

Preamble

After playing KSP for a while I wanted a tad more granularity to the building upgrades at the Kerbal Space Center. Going from 30 max parts to 255 in one upgrade felt a little too much all at once.

The stock game's four levels (initial + three upgrades) just seemed too basic, and very unKerbalistic. So I sat down with Bill and added some Komplexity and moar levels!

Yes, now there are a total of ten levels (initial plus nine upgrades) for every building in the KSC. This add-on depends on Module Manager and Custom Barn Kit by @Sarbian. Might not play nice with Bureaucracy.
