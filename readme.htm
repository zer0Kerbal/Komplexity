Komplexity (KPLX)

for Custom Barn Kit

by @zer0Kerbal

Kerbal Space Center building levels increased to a maximum of ten levels instead of the stock game's four.

Preamble

After playing KSP for a while I wanted a tad more granularity to the building upgrades at the Kerbal Space Center. Going from 30 max parts to 255 felt a little too much.

The stock game's four levels (initial + three upgrades) just seemed too basic, and very unKerbalistic. So I sat down with Bill and added some Komplexity and moar levels!

Yes, now there are a total of ten level (initial plus nine upgrades) for every building in the KSC. This mod depends on Module Manager and Custom Barn Kit by @Sarbian. Might not play nice with Bureaucracy.
